Enable WebVR

The button in the bottom right corner toggles a full-screen VR experience, allowing you to navigate and move using gestures.

Movement

Move around using the W, A, S, and D keys or with a controller. You can click and drag with the mouse to rotate your view.

Interactions

Interact with objects by looking at them with the reticule (targeting ring), or by tapping or clicking with the mouse.

Audio

Play a clip by interacting with a name. If you're having a hard time hearing someone, move closer.
